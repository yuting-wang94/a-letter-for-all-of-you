To All of You

Dear Reißig's family,


Having the chance to stay in Germany for almost 3 months has meant so much to me. Not only did I get to know the culture, food, lifestyle, but most importantly, I created some of the most unforgettable memories with you all.


It’s also crazy to think about how fast time passes. The first time I met Markus was only a few months after Dani and I got together. Then came the second time meeting Petra when we went hiking together in Japan, and later meeting Markus again — and of course, going out for drinks together once more😂
And now, here we are again, one and a half years later,
this time in Germany🇩🇪!!


Japan🇯🇵




Thank you for treating me like part of the family. I feel incredibly grateful! From having meals together, spending time talking, laughing, having BBQ and chilling in the garden together, to including me in family and company gatherings, and introducing me to your relatives and friends — every moment meant so much to me.

The way you show love and care, both through your actions and words, has touched me deeply. Even though I live so far away from my own family, being surrounded by such genuine warmth and hospitality made Germany feel like a second home


I feel so sad that it’s already time for me to leave …I truly enjoyed every moment with all of you! Thank you for giving me the opportunity to simply enjoy, experience and have fun. These memories will stay with me forever!


I hope to see you all soon!


Yuting
